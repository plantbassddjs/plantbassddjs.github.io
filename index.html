PLANT BASS'D DJs

Plant Bass'd DJs is a collective based in Co. Monaghan via Dublin and Edinburgh. We mix everything electronic that's groovy, funky and organic. We share our music discovery and create playlists that are updated with fresh tunes weekly.

This collective will strive to build a positive, fun team by bringing smooth beats to our sets. We are interested in not only mixing but producing music too and plan to expand into production.

You can contact us about any queries at plantbassdmgmt@gmail.com and check us out on Instagram at @plantbassddjs.
